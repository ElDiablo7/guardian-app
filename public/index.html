Frequently Asked Questions

How does the threat scanner work?

Paste any suspicious text, email, or chat log into the scanner. Our AI analyses it locally for grooming patterns, phishing indicators, and malicious URLs — without ever contacting the suspicious server.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We operate on a zero-knowledge model. Scanned content is processed in-session only and never stored on our servers. Your privacy is non-negotiable.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes. You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

What is the Digital Footprint Scanner?

It performs an OSINT-style scan of your digital presence — checking paste sites, social platforms, and public databases to show where your information is exposed online.
